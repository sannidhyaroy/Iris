Meet Iris!

The Search Experience to Blossom!

Iris provides a custom search engine experience that streamlines the way you search the web. It's not just another search engine, but rather a search tool that allows you to enjoy the beauty and diversity of all search engines and websites in one place.

One of the standout features of Iris is our custom background images, which feature stunning visuals, providing you a beautiful and inspiring backdrop for your searches.

With Iris' bangs, you can access a world of information and discover new things without having to limit yourself to just one search engine. Try Iris today and see the world of search in a whole new light.

What are Bangs?

Bangs are special characters which act as shortcuts to the web, that allow you to quickly search in different search engines, websites and services directly from the Iris Search Bar. By using a bang, you can search a website directly without having to visit it first. For example, if your search query contains a '!g' bang, Iris will automatically search for your query on Google. Similarly, if you use '!yt', you're actually searching videos on YouTube. With bangs, you can search multiple websites and services from a single search bar, making your online search experience faster and more efficient.
